Son Goku, born Kakarot, is the iconic, kind-hearted protagonist of the Dragon Ball Wiki franchise. Originally a violent Saiyan infant sent to conquer Earth, a severe head injury erased his destructive programming. He evolved into Earth's greatest defender, continually training to break his own limits.Loosely inspired by Sun Wukong from the Chinese epic Journey to the West, Goku made his debut as a monkey-tailed martial artist. Though possessing god-like power, he retains a cheerful, humble, and somewhat naive demeanor. He values simple pleasures, like eating mountains of food and engaging in combat against strong opponents.Goku's immense strength and mastery over energy attacks like the Kamehameha make him a formidable force in the universe. Through various arcs, he unlocks incredible transformations, most notably the iconic Super Saiyan form. Despite being an alien, he deeply loves his family, including his wife Chi-Chi and his sons Gohan and Goten. His relentless drive to master his craft and protect his loved ones drives the overarching narrative of the metaseries.
